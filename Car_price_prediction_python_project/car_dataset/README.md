## Dataset Description

The dataset used in this project contains detailed information about different car models and their technical specifications and describing various characteristics of each vehicle along with its market price.

The dataset helps analyze how different vehicle attributes influence the **price of a car**.

### Key Features in the Dataset

**Basic Information**

* car_ID – Unique identifier for each car
* CarName – Name of the car model

**Car Risk and Classification**

* symboling – Insurance risk rating of the car

**Engine and Performance**

* enginetype – Type of engine used
* cylindernumber – Number of cylinders in the engine
* enginesize – Size of the engine
* horsepower – Engine power output
* peakrpm – Maximum engine revolutions per minute

**Fuel Information**

* fueltype – Type of fuel used (gas or diesel)
* fuelsystem – Fuel system used in the car
* aspiration – Type of aspiration (standard or turbo)

**Car Body and Structure**

* doornumber – Number of doors
* carbody – Type of car body (sedan, hatchback, convertible, etc.)
* drivewheel – Type of drive system (FWD, RWD, AWD)
* enginelocation – Engine placement (front or rear)

**Car Dimensions**

* wheelbase – Distance between front and rear wheels
* carlength – Length of the car
* carwidth – Width of the car
* carheight – Height of the car


**Engine Mechanics**

* boreratio – Diameter of engine cylinder
* stroke – Length of piston movement
* compressionratio – Engine compression ratio

**Fuel Efficiency**

* city – Fuel efficiency in city driving
* highway – Fuel efficiency on highways

**Target Variable**

* price – Selling price of the car

### Purpose of the Dataset

This dataset is used to perform **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** to identify patterns and relationships between car specifications and their prices. The analysis helps understand which factors such as engine size, horsepower, fuel efficiency, and car dimensions have the greatest impact on car pricing.

The dataset can also be used for machine learning models to predict car prices based on vehicle features.
